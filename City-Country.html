Mumbai, India, is a huge, exciting city by the Arabian Sea, and it’s one of the most vibrant places in the world! It's known as the “City of Dreams” because many people move there to chase their dreams, especially in Bollywood, India's famous movie industry. In fact, Mumbai makes more movies each year than Hollywood! You can also visit the Gateway of India, a giant stone arch built to welcome kings and queens. Another fun fact: Mumbai is home to the famous dabbawalas, people who deliver homemade lunches to thousands of office workers using bicycles and trains without ever mixing up the orders!
